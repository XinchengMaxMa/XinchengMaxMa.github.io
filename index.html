Xincheng Max Ma

Xincheng Max Ma is a PhD Candidate in Information Systems at the Department of Information Systems, Business Statistics and Operations Management (ISOM), HKUST Business School.

His research lies at the intersection of AI for business and digital marketing, with a particular focus on how firms can leverage AI to engage users and create business value.

Research Interests

AI Marketing Human-AI Interaction Generative AI Recommender Systems Digital Marketing Field Experiments

Before academia, Xincheng spent five years in industry across roles including technical product manager, marketing manager, and strategy consultant at leading companies such as JD.com. He now continues to engage with industry by advising companies on AI for business and digital marketing through academic collaborations.

From industry to academia, he has been driven by curiosity about the fundamental mechanisms of business growth and the role of technology in shaping them.
