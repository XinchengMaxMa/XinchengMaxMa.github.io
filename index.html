I am a PhD Candidate in Information Systems at the Department of ISOM, HKUST Business School.

My research lies at the intersection of AI for business and digital marketing, with a particular focus on how to leverage AI to engage users and create business value.

Before academia, I spent five years in industry across roles including technical product manager, marketing manager, and strategy consultant at leading companies such as JD.com. I now continue to engage with industry by advising companies on AI for business and digital marketing through academic collaborations.

From industry to academia, I have always been driven by a deep curiosity about the fundamental mechanisms of business growth and the role of technology in shaping them.
